# Notation casdiloc
-------------------

\\( \begin{array}{c c}
    1 & 2  \\\
    3 & 4
\end{array} \\)

Eg: The determinant :

\\( \begin{array}{c c}
       \uparrow & -  \\\
       \downarrow & \uparrow
\end{array} \\)

is written as :

\\( \lt {14\bar{3}} | \\)

-------------------------------

and in my notation:

\\( \begin{array}{c c}
    a_2 & b_2  \\\
    a_1 & b_1
\end{array} \\)

and so the same det will become:

\\( \lt {\bar {a_1} a_2 b_1} | \\)

-------------------------------

Giving the inequality :

\\( \lt {\bar {a_1} a_2 b_1} | = + \lt {14\bar{3}} |  \\)
